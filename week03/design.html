Key Principles

Effective web design combines aesthetics with functionality. Good design guides users naturally through content, making information easy to find and understand. Typography, color, spacing, and visual hierarchy all work together to create a cohesive experience.

Consistency in design builds trust and familiarity. When users encounter predictable patterns, they can focus on content rather than figuring out navigation. This reduces cognitive load and improves overall usability.
